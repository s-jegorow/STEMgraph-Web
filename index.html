STEMgraph Quick Guide

Navigation:

  • Left mouse button + move: Rotate camera
  • Left click + drag: Move nodes
  • Scroll wheel: Zoom
  • Right click + drag: Pan view

Node interaction:

  • Click node: Show details
  • Completed lessons can be marked and appear blue
  • To-Do lessons can be marked and appear yellow

Additional keys:

  • Arrow Left: Go back one step
  • SPACE: Reset view
  • F1: View this help (duh)
